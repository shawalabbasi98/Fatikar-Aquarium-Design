# Fatikar Aquarium Design

## Project Overview
Fatikar Aquarium is a design project focused on creating a structured and visually appealing aquarium setup. The project includes the CAD design of the aquarium structure and its supporting components to ensure durability, stability, and easy assembly.

The design emphasizes proper dimensioning, structural balance, and practical manufacturing considerations.

---

## Project Goals
- Design a stable aquarium structure
- Ensure accurate dimensions for glass panels and frame
- Optimize the structure for easy assembly
- Maintain aesthetic and functional design

---

## Design Work
- 3D CAD modeling of aquarium structure
- Layout design for glass panels
- Frame and support structure design
- Dimensioning and technical detailing

---

## Manufacturing Considerations
- Proper support for aquarium water load
- Strong frame structure for stability
- Accurate dimensions for glass fitting
- Clean and simple assembly process

---

## Deliverables
- 3D CAD model
- Structural design layout
- Technical drawings
- Manufacturing-ready design files

---

## Design Challenges
- Ensuring structural stability under water load
- Maintaining accurate glass panel alignment
- Designing a strong yet lightweight frame
- Achieving a clean and practical design

- CAD models
- Technical drawings
